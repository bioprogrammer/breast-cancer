Cancer is a complex series of diseases distinguished by cell type, location, stage, and grade, but, by definition it is defined by cellular growth that, through a complex series of mechanisms, seeks to evade the innate defenses of the immune system, and in the case of a malignancy, proliferate throughout the body effectively disrupting the homeostatic physiology of organ systems and introducing the possibility of death to the affected organism or individual.
